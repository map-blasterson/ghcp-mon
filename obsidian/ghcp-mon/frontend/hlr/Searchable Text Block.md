Any sufficiently large rendered-text block in the dashboard offers an inline, cursor-driven search affordance — hover surfaces a `?/` hint, clicking activates a searchbox, typing highlights case-insensitive matches in place, and click / shift-click / Enter cycles between them — plus a controlled "truncate long content with click-to-expand" mode driven from the parent.
